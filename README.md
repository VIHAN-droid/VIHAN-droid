### About Me

Hi! I'm **Vihan Ahlawat**, a second-year B.Tech student at **IIIT Delhi** majoring in Computer Science and Engineering. Grounded in core CS theory — Algorithm Design, Theory of Computation, Discrete Mathematics, Probability, Linear Algebra, and Operating Systems — I approach programming with a structured, systems-first mindset.

I'm proficient in **Python, Java, C, C++, and SQL**, and enjoy applying these to build performant, scalable systems. Over the past year, I've worked on projects that span low-level systems engineering and modern AI:

- A **Mini Operating System** extending EGOS with an MLFQ scheduler, smart ELF loader, and a Unix-like shell with pipelines & POSIX threads
- A **University ERP** (MVC, MySQL, HikariCP) serving 150+ concurrent users with RBAC and BCrypt-secured auth
- **SmartWallet**, a full-stack digital wallet with P2P transfers, live crypto trading, SQL triggers, and row-level locking
- **ScriptSmith**, an agentic AI content pipeline (LangChain · LangGraph · ChromaDB · FAISS · Groq Whisper) built during my Gen-AI internship at **Tecorb Technologies**

I'm passionate about blending **algorithmic depth with practical engineering** — whether that's tuning a scheduler, designing a RAG pipeline, or grinding competitive programming. Currently exploring **Generative AI, Agentic systems, and Retrieval-Augmented Generation**, while sharpening DSA on LeetCode, Codeforces, CodeChef, and GFG (400+ problems solved).

Always open to research-driven engineering, backend roles, and interesting collaborations — feel free to reach out!
